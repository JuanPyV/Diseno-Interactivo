The project were focused on doing, appeared of the problematic obtained in evident situations, both from our family and friend's families,, with the coincidence that there was at least a single familiar that fell in this problem, alcoholism, or in a minor case, drinking in a uncontrolled way, making them commit mistakes and take bad decisions. Looking to use this page as a tool that allow us to help younger people have a bigger concience about the use of alcoholic beverages, we plan to convert it into a reference when someone looks for information or a place that they can use to look for help, so the number of addicts can be lessened and those that can't make choices because of a drunken state can take better care for themselves.

Purpose



Our purpose with this software will be of helping people with adiction to alcohol, so they can in form themselves better on the risks of this path, and giving them a tool that can help them control their habits. We're motivated to know that our webpage will be able to gran a tool to those persons that want to fix themselves but don't have the capacity or self-control to do it, or at least giving them a way to ask for help in case they decide to keep onto their habits. After we've finished this project, we'll have adquired experience in realizing an inmersive software that can let the user interact freely and change their mentality so their bad habits will be controlled.

Planned reach



The estimated rach of this project, during an early stage, is to attract people from this country, Mexico, from an age sufficiently young all the way to older people that need a solution to the problem they're currently facing. The most basic part of this page, is offering all kinds of informations to the user about consequences, history, risks they're taking every time they get drunk, advice on how much you could drink without developing a problem. This last point, would be obtained by having the user giving us some data related to their capacity for this kind of beverages, weight, stature, and if they have any close familiar that might give them a propensity for developing this addiction. In other kind of data, all kinds of contacts that can help the user like associations with enough expertise to assit this people, or any kind of group that can help these people share their experiences or find others that have beenthorugh this kind of problem and can give all sorts of advices to other people.
