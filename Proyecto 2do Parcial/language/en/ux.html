Interest

The interest our persona has, are, firstly (if they're accesing our webpage) to find help, inform themselves, or try and locate a certain group that might be able to help them. What they're interested in is trying to find a way to drink safely, or at least find something that can help them control themselves when drinking, if they have a problem with that. Another thing that our persona wants to have is an easy access webpage that can let them know everything they want with a simple menu that contains all the content, so they don't have to investigate in the whole page, meaning thay they would be happy simply having tabs that lead them to all the content

Key goals

The user key goals if visiting this page, would be to make better choices regarding their habits, contacting people who can help get over that problem, informing themselves on the dangers of alcoholism, and using our application to drink safely.

A day in the life

A common day in the life of our user, would split into two: the first, an user who drinks alcohol regularly and has a heavy problem regarding that, and his day would go on normally, probably at work or on their usual activities, but in their case at some hours they would do heavy drinking, making them drunk; our goal is to let them approach our page with advertising small campaigns about the dangers of alcohol, and offering them solutions to attract them. Our second type of user, someone that only drinks on a few days, goes on his normal day, and gets attracted to this site by publicity about checking all the information you should know when drinking alcoholic beverages, they would probably visit this page, we would offer them all the information they may want, show them the system we've integrated and letting them know they should control this kind of habit.
